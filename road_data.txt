On Tuesday morning, Delhi Traffic Police said, “Singhu, Auchandi, Piau Maniyari, Saboli and Mangesh borders are closed. Please take alternate route via Lampur Safiabad, Palla and Singhu school toll tax borders. Traffic has been diverted from Mukarba and GTK road. Please avoid Outer Ring Road, GTK Road and NH-44.”

Tikri, Dhansa borders are closed for any traffic movement. Jhatikara border is open only for Light Motor Vehicles, two-wheelers and pedestrian movement.
